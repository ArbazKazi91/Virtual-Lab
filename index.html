Theory

Theory

The natural water content also called the natural moisture content is the ratio of the weight of water to the weight of the solids in a given mass of soil. This ratio is usually expressed as percentage.

Outcomes

Learning Outcomes

  • Determine the water content of a soil sample using the oven drying method.
    • Calculate water content accurately from laboratory observations and measurements. Interpret the effect of soil moisture content on the engineering behavior of soils such as compaction, strength, and settlement.
Procedure

Procedure

  1. Take empty container.
  2. Measure W1.
  3. Add wet soil.
  4. Measure W2.
  5. Dry in oven.
  6. Measure W3.
  7. Calculate water content.
